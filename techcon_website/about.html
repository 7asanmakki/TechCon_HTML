Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, hosting over 10,000 attendees annually.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. We bring together innovators, entrepreneurs, and technologists to share knowledge and drive the industry forward.
